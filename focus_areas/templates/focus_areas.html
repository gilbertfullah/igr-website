{% for par in parliamentary %}

Overview

{{ par.overview }}

Importance of Economics Advocacy

{{ par.importance }}

Initiatives by IGR

  • Tracking Legislative Performance:
    The IGR monitors the performance of parliamentarians, including their attendance, participation in debates, voting records, and engagement with constituents. Through comprehensive data analysis and reporting, the IGR provides citizens with valuable insights into the effectiveness of their elected representatives, encouraging transparency and fostering citizen accountability.
  • Legislative Research and Analysis:
    The IGR conducts research and analysis on legislative processes, bills, and policy proposals. They provide unbiased assessments, highlighting the potential impact and implications of proposed legislation. The IGR's research outputs inform public discourse, empower citizens to engage in informed debates, and contribute to evidence-based policy recommendations.
  • Citizen Education and Awareness:
    Recognizing the importance of an informed citizenry, the IGR conducts citizen education programs and awareness campaigns to enhance understanding of the legislative processes and the role of parliament. These initiatives aim to empower citizens to actively engage with their representatives, participate in public hearings, and provide input on policy matters.
  • Strengthening Oversight Mechanisms:
    The IGR advocates for the establishment and strengthening of oversight mechanisms within parliament. They promote transparency, accountability, and checks and balances by encouraging the establishment of committees, enhancing the role of the Public Accounts Committee, and facilitating mechanisms for citizen engagement in oversight processes.
  • Engaging Civil Society Organizations (CSOs):
    The IGR collaborates with civil society organizations to foster coordinated efforts in monitoring parliament and promoting citizen engagement. By building the capacity of CSOs, facilitating knowledge-sharing, and encouraging collaboration, the IGR strengthens the collective voice of civil society in advocating for transparent and accountable legislative processes.
  • Policy Advocacy:
    The IGR engages with parliamentarians, government officials, and stakeholders to advocate for reforms in legislative processes and procedures. Through evidence-based research, policy briefs, and direct engagement, the IGR highlights areas for improvement, proposes legislative reforms, and advocates for transparent and inclusive policymaking.
  • Citizen Feedback Mechanisms:
    The IGR promotes the establishment of citizen feedback mechanisms within parliament, enabling citizens to provide feedback, raise concerns, and offer suggestions on legislative matters. This feedback loop ensures that the voices of citizens are heard and considered during the decision-making process.
