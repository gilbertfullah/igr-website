Who Are We?

Institute for Governance Reform (IGR) is an independen national think-tank working with ordinary citizens and a variety of stakeholders to bring high quality evidence into policy making and implementation processes.

Learn More

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between knowledge and policy.

Our Vision

To become a respected policy voice in the African Sub-Region supporting public and private institutions to respond to the challenges of economic development and state effectiveness.

Core Values

  • Professionalism and Integrity
  • Quality
  • Neutrality and Independence
  • Accountability and Openness

What We Do?

Political Governance

Our work on political governance focuses on elections, promoting inclusivity, integrity and accountability of public institutions and bringing citizen's voices and views into policy processes.

Economic Advocacy

Our economists offer world-class expertise to bring efficiency in revenue generation and public spending. We track public sector audit, monitor state contracts and undertake political economy analysis to inform decision making.

Social Development

We focus our efforts on human capital building and work in partnership with coalitions and networks to make education, health and agricultural services benefit the poor and most marginalized including remote areas, women, youth and persons living with disability.

Parliamentary Monitoring

Our monitors work to ensure that both Parliament and Parliamentarians’ work is brought to the people to make parliament more open and responsive to the priorities of voters.

ICT for Open Governace

Our Tech for Open Governance platform provide space for government-citizen interaction. We empower citizens to track government services, monitor procurement, submit complaints, vote on issues and have a voice in public decision making.

Analytics

Our data analytics unit utilised credible primary data to identify trends and patterns, correlations and relationships, predictive modelling for public education and for making data-driven decisions.
